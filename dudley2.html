DUDLEY No.2 PATH

FACTS AND HISTORY ABOUT DUDLEY CANAL NO.2 PATH

Dudley No.2 Path is roughly 5.5 miles in length and the only path without any locks! However we can find black-and-white bridges dotted along the walk to remind us of the industrial history around the area. The path in connected to the Dudley no.1 Path found by the Blowers Green Lock.

The walk leads up to the nature reserve known as The Bumble Hole. Here the once-industrial landscape has been reborn as a tranquil haven with grassland, ponds, wild flowers and birds. It is a great place for walkers and cyclists to explore, and the visitor centre is run by the local community. We can also find the Netherton Tunnel, which is the LAST EVER canal tunnel ever built in Britain.
